Additional App Information:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Login/Signup Validation:
First and Last Name:
    - Letters from the English Alphabet only （i.e. no 中文, 한글， etc..)
    - can and must start with either capital or non-capital letter
    - only special characters ' \s are allowed
    - minimum of 2 characters and a maximum of 35 characters

Email:
    - for simplicity, only validating for common emails
        - @outlook.com -- 12 characters -- user part: 3
        - @gmail.com -- 10 characters -- user part: 5
        - @yahoo.com -- 10 characters -- user part: 5
        - @bcit.ca (of course) - 8 characters -- user part: 7
        - @iCloud.com -- 11 characters -- user part: 4
        - @hotmail.com -- 12
    - email must be at least 15 characters long
    - must contain only one @ characters
    - user part must start with a letter
        - can end in a number or letter
        - can contain a . or _  anywhere but the first and last characters
    - not case sensitive

Password:
    - must be at least 8 characters long
    - must contain at least one lower case letter
    - must contain at least one upper case letter
    - must contain at least one number 
    - must contain at least one of the following special characters ! # ^ * ?

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Product Validation

Title:
    - only letters and numbers are allowed 
    - special characters , ! ' \s : . / ( ) - | are also allowed 
    - max character limit of 150

Description:
    - only letters and numbers are allowed 
    - special characters , ! ' \s : . / ( ) - | are also allowed 
    - max character limit of 250

Price:
    - accepts up to 9999999.99
